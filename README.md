# Magento 2 Product Inquiry Extension

[Magento 2 Product Inquiry](https://www.ulmod.com/magento-2-product-inquiry-extension.html) is a Magento 2 extension that allows you to add an "inquiry request" option in the product and category page where buyers can submit any related questions they have regarding your product.

With Magento 2 Product Inquiry, you can enable inquiry option to all products, categories, or per a particular product and category. 

<a href="https://www.ulmod.com/magento-2-product-inquiry-extension.html"><img width="205" src="https://www.ulmod.com/media/wysiwyg/um-download-now1.png"></a>

## Key Features

* Ability to enable inquiry to all products, categories
* Ability to enable inquiry option to specific product and category
* Ability to display inquiry form in the popup and new page. Support up to 4 layout pages to display the inquiry form on the new page
* Support  optional fields in the inquiry form, such as product name, product image, product SKU, telephone, attachment, and subject
* Support privacy policy (GDPR) field in the inquiry form for GDPR compliance
* Support up to four additional fields in the form with text, checkbox, and text area types
* Work with all product types
* Support autoresponder email notifications to buyers when an inquiry is submitted
* Support  email notifications to admins whenever buyers submit a new inquiry
* Support Google reCAPTCHA to avoid spam or bots inquiries
* Mobile-friendly
* Support multiple-stores
